HOOKS:
#####
  Hooks let you run actions at specific points in a release lifecycle...

Hook                            When it runs
####                            ############
pre-install                        Before resources are created
post-install                       After all resources are ready
pre-upgrade / post-upgrade         Around upgrades
pre-rollback / post-rollback       Around rollbacks
pre-delete / post-delete           Around uninstall  

Example:
*******  annotations:
            "helm.sh/hook": pre-install
            "helm.sh/hook-weight": "0"
            "helm.sh/hook-delete-policy": hook-succeeded
